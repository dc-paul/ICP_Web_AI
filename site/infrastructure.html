§ 01 · Internet Computer Node Provider

42 nodes.
Three data centers.

AN1 Antwerp · Datacenter United
BR1 Brussels · Digital Realty
BR2 Brussels · AtlasEdge

The Internet Computer (IC) is a blockchain-based extension of the public internet — a decentralized cloud where full-stack applications run directly on the protocol, without traditional servers or hosting providers. Allusion operates the hardware that makes that possible inside Belgium.

Networks rely on servers to process and store data. Industrial server hardware for the IC is run in top-level data centers to ensure maximum availability and the highest performance. We were the first Belgian Node Provider to onboard in May 2021, and have grown to 42 active nodes across three locations.

The infrastructure is intentionally over-provisioned: redundancy across data centers, dual-rack capacity, dedicated network paths. Decentralization without industrial discipline is a slogan, not a system.

§ 02 · Aviate Labs

Operational guardrails
as a service.

NMaaS Node Monitor as a Service
Cloud Engines SEV-SNP hardware pilots
HORUS Maritime / dual-use Web3 consortium

Aviate Labs is the operational arm — a research-and-services lab building the tooling that lets enterprises trust and scale decentralized networks. The core product, Node Monitor as a Service (NMaaS), gives Node Providers across the Internet Computer ecosystem comprehensive monitoring and support so they can focus on their main operations.

The lab also sits on the academic advisory boards of two Flemish research projects led by KU Leuven DistriNet and VUB Software Languages Lab — SODISA (blockchain/Web3, SBO/FWO funded) and CodeGuard (AI code assistants, VLAIO COOCK+ approved March 2026).

Aviate is also the operational partner on HORUS, a five-party maritime and dual-use Web3 consortium with a demonstrator scheduled at BlueBridge Ostend in October 2026.
