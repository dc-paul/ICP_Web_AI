§ 02 · Approach

A sparring partner,
not a passenger.

Who we back

Europe-first founders who are globally curious — building infrastructure for a sovereign, healthier, and better future.

Stage

Pre-seed and seed. We write first checks.

Ticket

€50K — €400K first ticket · follow-ons up to €5M.

CLA rounds

Big supporter of bringing the speed of convertible-loan rounds to Europe — and of helping you run one well.

How we help

Sparring on product and go-to-market. We love the first growth phase from discovery to repetitive business — and then make ourselves redundant as fast as possible.

What we avoid

Hardware-only plays without recurring software upside. SPV-stacking. Valuations untethered from current traction. We pass often and we say why.
